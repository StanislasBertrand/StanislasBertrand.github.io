RetinaFace-tf2

Retinaface-tf2 is a Tensorflow2.0+ reimplementationn of the RetinaFace paper (RetinaFace: Single-stage Dense Face Localisation in the Wild), state of the art face detector, with pretrained weights available.

UniversalFaceRecog-TF2

UniversalFaceRecog-TF2 is a Tensorflow2.0+ framework to recognize the faces of celebrities in images. Will include a set of pretrained celebrities, as well as pipeline to easily add new people to be recognized.
